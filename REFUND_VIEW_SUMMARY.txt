## 🎬 STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

STAFF - HOW TO ISSUE A REFUND:
───────────────────────────────
1. Go to Staff Dashboard
2. Look for the GREEN card labeled "REFUNDS"
3. Click it
4. You see:
   • Search box for student
   • Amount field
   • Reason field
5. Type student name/ID in search
6. Select from dropdown
7. Enter amount (e.g., 500.00)
8. Enter reason (e.g., "Double charged")
9. Click "Issue Refund" button
10. See success message with new balance
11. Refund appears in history below

STUDENT - HOW TO VIEW YOUR REFUNDS:
────────────────────────────────────
1. Go to Wallet
2. Scroll down to see all cards
3. Find the GREEN card "MY REFUNDS"
4. Click it
5. You see:
   • Total refunded amount
   • Number of refunds
   • List of all refunds
   • Who refunded you (staff name)
   • Why you were refunded (reason)
   • Date & time
   • Amount with ₱ symbol
